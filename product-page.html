Description

Our own little spot of sunshine the Cyclone Coat is a great example of a classic, timeless design with modern execution. This coat will keep you dry with its 15,000mm waterproof shell membrane and the patent-pending quick-dry coating lets you get rid of 99% of the water with just a few shakes.

An ideal coat that can handle most environments while being environmentally friendly with mostly recycled materials. The fabrics are 2-way stretch ensuring a full range of mobility without any discomfort and let you enjoy a wide range of activities without limits.

    Materials

  • Outer fabric: 70% recycled polyester, 30% elastan
  • Inner lining: 50% recycled wool, 50% recycled cotton
  • Phone-pocket: PVC (waterproof up to 20 meters underwater)
  • Zipper: YKK nylon zipper (waterproof)
  • Snap fasteners: stainless steel, corrosion reistant

    Technical information

  • Patent-pending quick-dry coating on the outer fabric
  • Sturdy 2-way waterproof YKK zipper at the front with a rashguard to prevent discomfort and scratching
  • All seams are taped
  • Adjustable elastic in the lining at the wrists, waist, hood and lower edge
  • Two spacious sidepockets
  • Inner waterproof pocket ideal for phone/electronics which maintains full touch-screen functionality or for important documents like passport/tickets
